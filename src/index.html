Tokyo 2020

Swimming at the Summer 2020 Olympics

Events

The Summer 2020 Olympic Games were delayed on year due to the COVID-19 Pandemic. A total of 37 swimming events took place from July 24th through August 1st.

Of the 37 contested events there were events 18 for men, 18 events for women, and 1 mixed event.

Three new events, Men's 800 Free, Women's 1500 Free, and Mixed 4x100 Medley relay, were contest at the Olympic level for the first time.

Highlights

A tight 100 free race that comes down to the wire.
Ahmed Hafnaoui snags Gold for Tunisia in an outside smoke.
A new World Record and the first such record of the games.
China's 4x200 Free relay upsets the Americans and Australians for Gold and a WR.
Chase Kalisz takes gold in what was Michael Phelps best event, the 400 IM.
